About me

I turn ideas into experiences

Hi, I'm Treasure a UI/UX Designer and Junior Frontend Developer from Kampala, Uganda. I believe great design is not just about how things look, but how they feel and work for the people using them.

I'm currently the Design Lead on a relationship platform, a purpose driven platform helping serious adults find meaningful, lasting love. I participated in the full design process from research and wireframes to high fidelity prototypes and design systems.
